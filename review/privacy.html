Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 26, 2026

We collect only the data needed to authenticate TikTok accounts and support uploads through user-initiated actions.

1. Data we collect

  • OAuth tokens and TikTok account identifiers required for API access.
  • Video file metadata related to uploads performed by the user.
  • Usage metadata needed for security and audit logs.

2. How we use it

  • Authenticate with TikTok during explicit login flows.
  • Complete upload actions initiated by you.
  • Investigate errors and protect service integrity.

3. Storage and retention

  • Tokens are encrypted in storage and not shared with unrelated parties.
  • Data is kept only while required to operate your connected workflow.

4. External services

We use TikTok API endpoints for OAuth and upload operations in accordance with TikTok platform policy.

5. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the support channel listed in the app interface.
